Personal Loan App Interest rate and charges

Here are our affordable interest rates and charges levied on our offerings

Interest Rate

Our competitive interest rates make borrowing affordable and stress-free. The personal loan interest rates start from min 17% - max 29.95% p.a.

Loan Processing Charges

The loan processing charges are levied to cover the cost platforms incur while processing the loan. KreditBee charges processing fees ranging from ₹85 to up to 6.5% of the loan amount.

Penal interest

Penal interest is charged at the loan interest rate over and above the normal interest levied on a loan.

Onboarding Fee for New Customers

The onboarding fee for new customers is a standard, nominal fee that is levied one-time for all first-time users. This fee is charged when you take your first loan.

GST on Onboarding and Processing Fee

GST is charged on all loan fees at 18%

Loan Cancellation

For a personal Cash Loan, once your loan is approved and disbursed, it cannot be cancelled. You will be required to repay the amount in full in order to close the loan. For a purchase finance loan, loan cancellation may be possible upon confirmation of the merchant after return of product.
